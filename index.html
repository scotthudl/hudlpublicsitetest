Hudl.cloud: property management, simplified

Hudl.cloud provides a simple, elegant solution to managing your property portfolios.
From property owners to trustees to managing agents, Hudl.cloud connects them all.

WORKS ON ALL DEVICES

Hudl.cloud is web-based an works on devices from the smallest smartphone to the largest laptop.

CLOUD-BASED MANAGEMENT

Manage all aspects of your property portfolio on a centralized, cloud-based platform. Your data is available wherever you go.

CONSTANT IMPROVEMENT

The Hudl.cloud platform is constantly improved based on customer feedback - you gain the benefit of new features suggested by other users.

Minutes in Seconds

Hudl.cloud simplifies the organization, minuting and storage of meeting agendas and minutes. The system handles follow-ups on meeting tasks so you don't have to.

Capture minutes on a central platform that is easy to use and understand.

Automatically generate and distribute elegant PDFs with your company branding included.

Centralized Documents

Hudl.cloud includes a centralized storage system for all of your property-specific files. Share, manage and track your files with ease.

Simple Task Management

Hudl.cloud manages relevant tasks across all players in your property portfolio. Tasks can be viewed and managed on a high-level overview or in fine-grain detail.

Organize, allocate and take notes on individual tasks. Upload files to individual tasks, and use built-in checklists to track progress.

Tasks can be grouped under categories and projects for easy organization.
